✨ GLOBAL DESIGN PRINCIPLES

Typography: Mix a heavy display font (like Syne, Clash Display, or Space Grotesk Bold) for headlines + Inter for body. Use large scale contrast — huge headlines next to tiny labels.
Spacing: Generous. Sections breathe. Nothing feels crowded.
Glassmorphism: Used selectively — navbar, stat cards, quote cards only. Not overused.
Glow / Aura: Green glow used as a lighting metaphor — appears behind key elements, on hover states, on CTA buttons. Not neon-garish, but cinematic.
Motion hints: Annotate in Figma where animations would occur — floating nodes, counter, marquee, card hover lifts.
Section numbering: Use / 01, / 02 etc. as small decorative labels to give editorial structure.
Mobile: Every section collapses gracefully. Hero text scales down. Bento grid goes single column. Navbar becomes hamburger with full-screen overlay menu.
